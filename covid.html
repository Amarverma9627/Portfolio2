1) Prevention from COVID-19
Wash your hands often with soap & water, and/or rub alcohol-based sanitizers, for at least 20 seconds. Stay at home as much as possible. Wear gloves and face masks while going out. Maintain a safe distance from a coughing or sneezing person. Avoid touching your eyes, nose, or mouth.
2) How is COVID treated?
Most people with COVID-19 have mild illness and can recover at home. You can treat symptoms with over-the-counter medicines, such as acetaminophen or ibuprofen, to help feel better.
